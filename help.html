1. Account & Login

How do I create an account?

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button at the top right of the page. Fill in your details, including name, email address, and password, then click "Create Account." You'll receive a confirmation email to verify your email address.

How do I reset my password?

If you've forgotten your password, go to the login page and click on "Forgot Password." Enter your email address, and we’ll send you instructions to reset your password.

How can I update my profile information?

Log into your account and navigate to "My Account." You can update your name, email, shipping address, and payment methods from there.

2. Shopping & Orders

How do I browse and search for products?

You can browse our products by category using the navigation menu. Alternatively, use the search bar at the top of the page to find specific items. You can filter results by size, color, price, and more.

How do I add items to my cart?

Simply click on a product to view more details. From the product page, select your desired size and color (if applicable), and then click "Add to Cart."

How can I apply a discount code?

On the checkout page, you’ll see a field labeled "Discount Code" or "Promo Code." Enter your code and click "Apply" to have the discount reflected in your total.

What should I do if my order is missing items?

If your order arrives with missing items, please contact our support team immediately at fakeluxesupport@luxe.gmail.com or via the "Contact Us" page.

Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it?

Once an order is placed, it is processed quickly to ensure fast shipping. Unfortunately, we cannot make changes or cancellations once an order is confirmed. However, you can return most items for a refund or exchange.

3. Payment & Billing

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit and debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and Apple Pay.

How can I add or update my payment method?

You can update your payment method in your account under "Payment Methods." Add a new card or remove an old one.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes! We use SSL encryption to ensure that your payment information is securely transmitted. Our payment processing is compliant with the latest security standards.

Can I use multiple payment methods in one order?

Currently, we only accept one payment method per order.

4. Shipping & Delivery

How much is shipping?

Shipping costs depend on your location and the shipping method you choose. You can view shipping fees at checkout before finalizing your order.

How long will it take to receive my order?

Standard shipping typically takes 5-7 business days. Expedited shipping options are available during checkout for faster delivery.

Can I track my order?

Once your order has shipped, you’ll receive an email with a tracking number. You can track your package through our carrier’s website.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we offer international shipping to select countries. Shipping rates and delivery times vary by location.

What happens if my order is delayed or lost?

If your order is delayed or lost, please contact us immediately. We will assist you in tracking the package or issuing a replacement.

5. Returns & Exchanges

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of receipt for most items. To qualify for a return, the product must be unused, in its original packaging, and with the receipt.

How do I initiate a return or exchange?

Visit our "Returns & Exchanges" page, where you can complete a return request form. We’ll provide you with instructions on how to send back the item.

How long do I have to return an item?

Returns must be initiated within 30 days of receiving your order.

Can I return sale or clearance items?

Sale or clearance items are generally final sale, but please check the specific product page for any exceptions.

6. Product Information

How do I know if a product is in stock?

If a product is out of stock, the website will display a "Sold Out" label on the product page. You can sign up to receive an email notification when the item is back in stock.

What should I do if an item is out of stock?

You can check back later or sign up to be notified when the product becomes available again.

How do I get more details about a product?

Each product page contains detailed descriptions, sizing information, and high-quality images. If you need further assistance, you can contact us directly.

7. Gift Cards & Vouchers

How do I purchase a gift card?

To buy a gift card, visit our "Gift Cards" page and select the amount you’d like to purchase. You can send it to the recipient via email or have it delivered to you.

How do I redeem a gift card or voucher?

During checkout, enter the gift card or voucher code in the "Apply Discount" section, and the value will be deducted from your order total.

Can I use a gift card on sale items?

Yes, gift cards can be used on sale items unless stated otherwise in the terms and conditions.

8. Customer Support

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support team by email at fakeluxesupport@luxe.gmail.com or by using our live chat feature on the website.

What are your support hours?

Our support team is available Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM (EST). We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours.

How can I report an issue with my order?

If you encounter an issue with your order, please contact us immediately with your order number and a description of the issue. We will do our best to resolve it as quickly as possible.
